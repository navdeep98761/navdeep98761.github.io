Types of Chihuahuas

How Are Chihuahuas Classified? There are six different types of Chihuahua, and some breed enthusiasts include the fawn Chihuahua as a seventh type. Determining which type of Chihuahua you have depends on the length of their fur and the shape of their head. Their size can also be a factor in their classification. The 6 Different Chihuahua Types

1.Short-Haired Chihuahua
Origin : Mexico
Defining Trait: Short, glossy coat
Lifespan : 14–16 years
Height : 5–8 inches
2.Long-Haired Chihuahua
Origin : Mexico
Defining Trait: Long, fluffy coat
Lifespan : 14–16 years
Height : 5–8 inches
3.Apple-Head Chihuahua
Origin : Mexico
Defining Trait: Well-rounded head with a short nose and large eyes
Lifespan : 14–16 years
Height : 5–8 inches
4.Deer-Head Chihuahua
Origin : Mexico
Defining Trait: Elongated head with a long, narrow snout
Lifespan : 14–16 years
Height : 5–8 inches
5.Pear-Head Chihuahua
Origin : Mexico
Defining Trait: A combination of apple and deer head types, with a flatter skull
Lifespan : 14–16 years
Height : 5–8 inches
6.Teacup Chihuahua
Origin : Mexico
Defining Trait: No more than 6 inches tall or more than 5 pounds
Lifespan : 7–12 years
Height : 6 inches
